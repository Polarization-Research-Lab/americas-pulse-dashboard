\hypertarget{about}{%
\section{About}\label{about}}

We are a cross-university research lab (Dartmouth College, University of
Pennsylvania and Stanford University). The lab exists to serve as a
nexus for work on affective polarization, social trust, and political
violence.

Our lab focuses on addressing the following critical questions:

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  What are the principal causes of affective polarization and what can
  be done to treat it?
\item
  When and where does affective polarization alter behavior? Why?
\item
  How effective and durable are approaches to reducing affective
  polarization? \newpage
\end{itemize}
